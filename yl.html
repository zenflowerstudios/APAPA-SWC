Youth Leadership Programs

Build skills, shape characters, and raise values with our intern and junior programs.

Internship

The APAPA Seattle Chapter provides an internship opportunity to prepare high school and college students to become future leaders. This program provides an opportunity for students to develop their leadership skills, gain public policy experience, and become civically engaged. The internship program will develop:

  • Political awareness and civic engagement
  • Understanding of government and legislative processes
  • Professional and leadership qualities
  • Networking and Team work skills with other fellow interns

Requirements:

  • Membership is required.
  • Must be able to drive.
  • At least 100+ hours with your job placement.
  • Attend all training sessions outside internship hours.

Incentives:

  • Interns will recieve a stipend for successful completion of the program. In 2018, the stipend is $800.
  • Parties and field trips!
  • Interns may be selected to attend the Vision New America Conference, an international youth leadership program held in Berkeley, California, with all expenses paid.

Junior Program

The junior program encourages younger volunteers and their friends and families to be engaged in community events and volunteering opportinies year-round.

Requirements:

  • Membership is required.
  • No experience is necessary.
  • Applicants must be 13 years of age and over.
  • Flexible hours and is interest-driven

Incentives:

  • Priority consideration for the internship
  • Parties and field trips!
