Membership

Give back to the program, community, and your growth

Membership Benefits

  • Eligible to apply for internship.
  • Members who have gone through the junior program have priority to apply for internship.
  • Priority access to limited volunteer opportunities.
  • Members may get selected to attend the APAPA annual international leadership conference in Berkeley, California, all expenses paid.
  • Parties and field trips!

Membership Details

  • Initial annual enrollment is $100 per person
  • Family members get a 10% discount for the initial annual membership fee.
  • Applicants who qualify for free or reduced lunch will qualify for a free or reduced membership fee.
  • The annual membership renewal fee is $25 per member.

Steps to Apply

  1. Fill in your information on the Google Form.


  2. Pay the Membership Fee, either through the link on the Google Form or through the Paypal Button Below.
