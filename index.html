Our Vision

As a grassroots, community-based, non-profit, non-political, and non-partisan organization, APAPA Seattle Chapter focuses on youth leadership programs, community outreach, and volunteer opportunities to increase civic engagement and nurture leadership.

Youth Leadership Programs

In addition to the traditional internship which APAPA provides nationwide, the Seattle Chapter is proud to present the junior program for younger volunteers.

Community Partnership and Outreach

We collaborate with established community organizations and leaders to bring forth various civic missions while engaging youth and families to build a better community.

Volunteering Opportunities

We work with community organizations and engage our youth to establish civic responsibilities and leadership.

Join our community

We are a 100% volunteer-based organization that strives to increase Asian Pacific Islander civic participation through youth leadership programs, community outreach, and volunteering opportunities. Please consider contributing and joining us to give back to the program, the community, and your growth.
